 Year of formation : 1958, April 1 Headquarters : Kakkanad Major Rivers : Periyar, Muvattupuzha Ernakulam has largest number of Taluks (7)  District with highest per capita income.  District with more towns  Ernakulam is the first 'Child friendly' district in India  Ernakulam District has the largest number of industrial units in Kerala. Ernakulam Aluva belt is a major industrial zone.  The first Stock Exchange of Kerala began to function at Kochi in 1978.  The port of Kochi is known as the 'Queen of the Arabian Sea'.  Vasco da Gama's body was first buried in St. Francis Church, Kochi.  The seat of the High Court of Kerala is at Ernakulam.  Cochin port is the only major port in Kerala.  Robert Bristo is the man behind the construction of modern port of Kochi.  Shanmugam Chetty, the first finance minister of India called Kochi as 'Queen of Arabian Sea'.  Pallippurama fort built by Portuguese in 1503 is the first European fort in India.  Mangalavanam, the famous Bird Sanctuary situated in the heart of the Cochin city.  First successful heart transplantation in Kerala was held at Medical Trust Hospital by Dr. Jose Chacko Periapuram.  Kochi is the first e-port in India.  Ernakulam is the first fully literate district in India (1990)  Kumbalangy is the first tourism village in Kerala.  Speed post system was firstly introduced in Ernakulam.  Vallarpadam Terminal is the first International Container Transhipment Terminal of India.  First floating ATM in India is established in Kochi by SBI.  First multiplex theater in Kerala in at Kochi.  First Rubber park in India is at Irapuram.  India's first child friendly district is Ernakulam.  First tourism police station in India is located at Mattancherry in Ernakulam.  The first milk ATM in Kerala is in Ernakulam
