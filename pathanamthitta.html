Year of formation : 1982, November 1 Major rivers : Pamba, Achenkovil, Manimala Pathanamthitta is the 13th district in Kerala, carved out of Kollam and Alappuzha districts.  Pathanamthitta is the first district in India to show zero population growth. It is also the first polio free district in India.  The birth place of Padayani is Kadammanitta  Sabarigiri, Kakkad and Maniyar are the hydro-electric projects in the district.  Lord Ayyappa temple at Sabarimala, a famous pilgrim centre in South India is in the district.  Ranni is the biggest forest division in Kerala.  Konni elephant shed (Anakkottil) is situated here.  The famous Uthrattathi Snake Boat race is held in Aranmula in river Pamba.  The Parthasarathi temple is at Aranmula.  The largest religious convention of Asia is the Maraman Convention, held on the bank of river Pamba.  Veluthampi Dalawa became a martyr at Mannadi.  The Cherukolpuzha Hindu religious convention is also held on the bank of river Pamba.  Largest sugarcane cultivated in Thiruvalla taluk, which is in Pathanamthitta district. www.keralapscgk.com | Telegram Channel | Youtube | Facebook  The only railway station in Pathanamthitta is at Thiruvalla.
